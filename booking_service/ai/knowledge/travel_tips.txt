BUSGO TRAVEL TIPS AND PASSENGER GUIDE

Before Your Journey:
- Arrive at the boarding point at least 15 minutes early.
- Download your ticket or take a screenshot in case of poor internet connectivity.
- Carry a valid photo ID matching the name on your booking.
- Charge your devices before travel as not all buses have charging ports.
- AC Sleeper buses can get very cold at night. Carry a light blanket or request one from the operator.

Choosing the Right Bus:
- For overnight journeys, AC Sleeper buses offer the most comfortable experience.
- Volvo buses are best for medium distance routes with premium comfort.
- AC Seater buses are ideal for day journeys under 6 hours.
- Non-AC buses are the most economical option for short routes.
- Check the operator rating before booking. Operators with ratings above 4.0 are highly reliable.

During Your Journey:
- Keep your ticket handy throughout the journey for spot checks.
- Do not place valuable items in the overhead compartment.
- Inform the driver or conductor if you have a medical condition.
- Rest stops are usually scheduled every 3 to 4 hours on long routes.
- In case of bus breakdown, the operator is obligated to arrange an alternative bus within 2 hours.

Health and Safety Tips:
- Carry basic medicines like motion sickness tablets if needed.
- Stay hydrated during long journeys.
- Avoid heavy meals just before boarding.
- For senior citizens and pregnant passengers, lower berths and front seats are recommended.
- Emergency contact number for BusGo support is available in the app under Help section.
