BUSGO BOOKING RULES AND SEAT POLICIES

Booking Rules:
- Each passenger must have a valid booking with a unique seat number.
- A maximum of 6 seats can be booked in a single transaction.
- Bookings must be made at least 30 minutes before departure.
- Children below 5 years travel free but do not get a separate seat.
- Children between 5 and 12 years are charged 50% of the adult fare.

Seat Selection:
- Window seats are numbered 1, 4, 5, 8, 9 and so on in most buses.
- Sleeper buses have lower and upper berths. Lower berths are preferred for elderly passengers.
- Front seats (1 to 4) are recommended for passengers prone to motion sickness.
- Emergency exit rows have extra legroom but cannot be assigned to elderly or disabled passengers.

ID and Boarding:
- Passengers must carry a valid government-issued photo ID during travel.
- Boarding closes 10 minutes before departure. Late arrivals may forfeit their seat.
- The booking confirmation SMS or email must be shown at boarding.
- Name on booking must match the ID presented at boarding.

Luggage Policy:
- Each passenger is allowed one bag up to 15 kg in the luggage compartment.
- One small cabin bag up to 5 kg is allowed inside the bus.
- Excess luggage is charged at 20 rupees per kg.
- Prohibited items include flammable materials, weapons, and perishable food.
