BUSGO CANCELLATION AND REFUND POLICY

Full Refund Policy:
- Cancellations made more than 24 hours before departure receive a 100% refund.
- Cancellations made between 12 to 24 hours before departure receive a 75% refund.
- Cancellations made between 6 to 12 hours before departure receive a 50% refund.
- Cancellations made less than 6 hours before departure receive no refund.
- No-shows without prior cancellation receive no refund.

How to Cancel:
- Log in to your BusGo account and go to My Bookings.
- Select the booking you want to cancel and click Cancel Booking.
- Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days to the original payment method.

Special Cases:
- If the bus is cancelled by the operator, passengers receive a 100% refund automatically.
- In case of natural disasters or government-declared emergencies, full refunds are issued regardless of timing.
- Partial cancellations for group bookings are allowed up to 12 hours before departure.

Rescheduling Policy:
- Passengers can reschedule up to 12 hours before departure for a flat fee of 50 rupees.
- Rescheduling to a higher priced ticket requires payment of the fare difference.
- Only one reschedule is allowed per booking.
