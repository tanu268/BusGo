Bus Type Guide:
- AC Sleeper: Best for overnight journeys above 8 hours. Has fully reclining berths.
- Volvo Multi-Axle: Premium seating, good suspension, best for hilly routes.
- AC Seater: Comfortable for 4 to 8 hour day journeys.
- Non-AC Sleeper: Budget overnight option, comfortable berths without air conditioning.
- Non-AC Seater: Most economical, suitable for short routes under 4 hours.

Peak Travel Seasons:
- Summer holidays April to June see highest demand on all routes.
- Diwali and Holi festivals cause 2x to 3x surge in bookings.
- Book at least 3 days in advance during festive seasons.
- Weekends see higher demand than weekdays on most routes.
